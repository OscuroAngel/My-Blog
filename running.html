From I Hate Running to UltraMarathon

I bet you have said “I hate running” many times in your life. Everyone did. Except maybe few crazy ones but let's not talk about those exceptions. “Lifting weights faster is my Cardio” used to be my mojo when I just started gym. How the hell I ended up running Ultra….? I have no idea. But I will tell you my whole story of getting into this sport, in some other post. Now I will try to show you how I've been taking running wrong my whole life...

Mind Body Fitness

Imaging yourself at the start of a 100 mile race… the heart is rising. Many athletes around you. And in front of you are long hours of the trail full of struggle and inner war. If you are not elite and it's your first time on this distance, it might take you average of 40 hours to complete the race. During the race you will suffer from luck of sleep, energy, mental power and lot of pain. You may fall, you may get lost and eventually stop from racing.

Corfu Mountain Trail 2021

“Why would anybody want to run for 24 hours?” Honestly, I still don’t have a clear answer, guess I need to run more Ultras to find that out. What I know is that at least up to some kilometres I enjoy being out there so much, that want to move further to see what lies ahead....
