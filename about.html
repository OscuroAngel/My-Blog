This is me

Hi! I'm Anzhelika and i study psychology in Greece on the island of Crete.
Last 7 years during the summer time i show to the guests of the island local mountains and trails.
Since the end of 2018 I discovered for myself trail running and this changed my life. It became my passion and my lifestyle.
In one year of running i manage to finish my first Ultra Marathon - Ultra Trail Pilion 82km. My next goal at the moment is 100 miler - ROUT this October.

Runner, Photographer, Mountain leader

Apart of my running, last 10 years i have photography as a way to express myself and share my experiences. I used to work as an event photographer for 4 years in various venues in the town of Rethymno.
Lately i start to combine the two passions and photograph fellow runners.
Here you can also find some of my works from races.
Follow me and subscribe to see my progress toward my goal to run my first 100 miler. Also for beautiful view from races and mountains i visit.
Later you will be able to find also on this page articles on the topic of Running and Mental Health.
