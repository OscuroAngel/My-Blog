Welcome to my Blog

It was created as a place where i can share my story. Show to the visitors the beauty of the mountains. And hopfuly inspire to more people to connect with nature.

And if you are a runner, you can send me your story, if you are not a runner just yet, hope to inspire you to start and join an amazing community of runners.
